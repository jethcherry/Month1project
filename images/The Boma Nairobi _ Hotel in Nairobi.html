    The Boma Nairobi

    Welcome to the Boma Nairobi, where sophistication meets comfort in a contemporary retreat housed within stunning art nouveau architecture. Our 148 rooms and suites blend modern amenities with tranquil charm, offering a welcoming haven for both business and leisure travelers.

    Explore our versatile event spaces, accommodating up to 200 guests, making us an ideal choice for conferences and unforgettable celebrations. Immerse yourself in culinary experiences that transcend the ordinary, adding a flavorful dimension to your stay. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our dedicated team is poised to ensure your time with us is remarkable.
